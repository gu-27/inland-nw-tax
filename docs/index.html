About

Inland NW Tax & Legal was built around a simple idea: most people with tax or legal questions don't need a big firm — they need a smart, credentialed professional who will actually listen and give them a straight answer.

With a Juris Doctor (JD) and a Master of Accountancy with a concentration in Taxation, the expertise here sits at the intersection of tax law and accounting — a combination that very few professionals in the region can offer.

Whether you're an individual navigating a complicated return, a business owner with questions about structure and tax exposure, or someone who's received an IRS notice and doesn't know where to turn — this is the right first call.
